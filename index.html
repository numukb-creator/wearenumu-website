What Is Ethics Gate™?

Ethics Gate™ is Numu's proprietary framework that governs every AI agent we deploy. It's not a checklist we run once and forget. It's a living governance layer that operates continuously inside your digital workforce.

Built on three Islamic ethical principles — Sidq (truthfulness), Amanah (trustworthiness), and 'Adl (justice) — Ethics Gate™ ensures that every AI output, every data access, and every automated decision meets verifiable ethical standards.

Only 10% of AI consultancies offer any ethical governance framework. We built ours into every workflow.

From Principles to Production

Five implementation steps that turn ethical principles into operational governance.

1

Pre-deployment assessment

25-question diagnostic across 7 pillars before any agent goes live. Covers data handling, output accuracy, bias risk, privacy compliance, stakeholder impact, transparency, and override protocols.

2

Automated guardrails

Real-time bias detection, content screening, and compliance checks built into every agent workflow. Guardrails run continuously — not just at deployment.

3

Transparency logging

Every agent decision, data access, and output is logged for audit. Full traceability from input to output, with reasoning chains documented.

4

Human-in-the-loop triggers

Sensitive actions (publishing content, sending proposals, pricing decisions) require human approval via Slack/email. Escalation thresholds are configurable per client.

5

Quarterly re-certification

Ongoing governance review to ensure continued compliance. Updated scorecard, drift detection, and updated recommendations.

Decision Logic: Traffic Light System

Green — Approved

Output passes all three pillars. Deployed automatically. Logged for audit trail.

Yellow — Review

Potential concern flagged. Held for human review before deployment. Common for nuanced content or edge cases.

Red — Blocked

Output fails one or more pillars. Blocked from deployment. Flagged for remediation with specific violation details.

Built on six principles drawn from Islamic spirituality and refined through Peter Gould's Heart-Centered Design framework, Ihsaan Flow ensures that every phase of work — from initial brief to final delivery — meets the highest standard of intention, craft, and care.

Where Ethics Gate™ checks what we produce, Ihsaan Flow governs how we produce it. Together, they form Numu's complete ethical operating system.
